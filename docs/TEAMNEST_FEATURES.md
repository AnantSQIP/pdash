*TeamNest (teamnest.com) is a cloud-based HR and payroll automation SaaS for Indian SMBs, developed by Mumbai-based TESPL (also appearing as "Teamnest Employee Services Private Limited", founded 2017; founders Mahesh Jethani, Prashant Shah, Saumil Gandhi, Vishrut Srivastava, Pradeep Chauhan), with mobile apps published as "TeamNest HR Suite" — the name is effectively unique to this product.*

### Product Identity, Vendor & Market
- **Product identity**: TeamNest is a cloud-based (SaaS) HR and payroll automation platform for Indian small/medium businesses sold at teamnest.com; no significant competing software product named "TeamNest" exists (the closest similarly-named product is "HRnest", an unrelated Polish tool), and the "TeamNest HR Suite" mobile apps belong to the same product.
- **Vendor**: Developed by TESPL (CIN U93090MH2017PTC294505, PAN AAGCT2410G, GST 27AAGCT2410G1ZN), a Mumbai-based HR and technology company founded in 2017; the legal entity also appears as "Teamnest Employee Services Private Limited", and the product is promoted through the myitreturn.com tax-filing ecosystem (same corporate family, Skorydov).
- **Market footprint**: Site claims 450+ clients in 9 countries (India, Dubai/UAE, UK, Italy, Germany, Spain, France, Netherlands, Belgium) across 25–30+ industry verticals.
- **Certifications**: ISO 27001:2022 (information security) and ISO 20000-1:2018 (IT service management) certified.
- **Module line-up**: 14 modules in 4 categories — Time & Productivity (Attendance, Leaves, Timesheets, Roost task management), Payroll & Expenses (Payroll Engine, Business and Travel Expenses), Compliance (central and state statutory compliance with a calendar interface), and Employee Engagement (Onboarding, Helpdesk, Chatter/Engagement, Employee Self-Service, Exit Management, Performance Management System), plus an Investments module for tax-saving declarations feeding tax computation.

- **All modules in one plan**: Both plans include Attendance, Leaves, Expenses, Roost, Timesheet, Payroll, Compliance, Helpdesk, Engagement, and Employee Self-Service (PMS/Onboarding/Exit are not explicitly named in the pricing module list but no license gate is published for them).

### Platform, Hosting & Delivery
- **SaaS delivery**: Web-based with no installation, vendor-hosted and maintained; a "self signup cloud platform" with per-organization sign-in via a tenant-specific "team's Teamnest URL" and self-service domain registration.
- **Per-tenant subdomains & multi-tenant isolation**: Each customer gets its own domain (e.g., companyname.teamnest.com); multi-tenant architecture where "your Organization's data is secured in your own private location".
- **AWS hosting**: Data is stored on Amazon Web Services "for complete security of professional and personal information", alongside "industry standard security" and the ISO certifications.
- **Multi-device web support**: The web app runs on desktops, mobiles, iPads, and tablets; third-party listings cite Windows/macOS/Linux/Ubuntu/iOS/Android as supported platforms (web-based).
- **Public tax calculator tool**: A free income-tax calculator hosted at teamnest.com/tax-calculator/ (also surfaced via partner myitreturn.com).

### Core HR & Employee Database
- **Single employee database**: The entire HR information set is accessed in a single database — one system of record for all HR data with organizational policies integrated.
- **Employee profile records**: Stores each individual's academic records, organisational experience details, medical records, bank account details, and UID/PAN card details in organized profile sections.
- **Configurable ESS profile sections**: Profile data is categorizable into sections such as Personal, Educational, Organisational, Experience, Miscellaneous (also Accommodation, Onboarding, Reassignment), each with adjustable visibility/access settings.
- **Document repository**: One-stop store for all employee personal and professional documents, vouchers, and screenshots — lifecycle documents (personal, professional, payroll) accessible in a click.
- **Candidate Grid / Employee Grid**: Two-stage people register — pre-joining candidates live in a Candidate Grid and are migrated to the Employee Grid on confirmation, with access rights assigned at conversion.
- **Onboarding-to-exit record keeping**: Data maintained systematically from onboarding to exit, including background checks, onboarding tasks, appointment letters, and resignation/termination records.
- **HR letters**: Appointment/offer letters are generated/uploaded by Admin/HR and issued through the system (candidates view and accept letters online); termination and relieving letters are generated through the Utilities Module.
- **Reporting structure / org hierarchy**: Admins set up the reporting structure (Admin Section > Organization > Reporting Structure) defining who reports to whom; it drives approval hierarchy in workflows, and organization structures are user-definable.
- **Configurable approval workflows**: Approvals for Attendance, Leaves, and Expenses auto-route requests to designated approvers based on predefined rules; organizations create their own policies, timings, CTC structures, org structures, and approval workflows.
- **Master Policy framework**: Shift, attendance, leave, expense, helpdesk, weekly-off, holiday-calendar, and approval-workflow rules are bundled into a Master Policy (seven mandatory rule sections) and assigned to individual employees, an Org Unit, or an Org Group via the Employee Grid / Apply Policies.
- **Bulk data import (Setup File)**: A structured "Teamnest Setup File" spreadsheet template initializes the portal with employee master data.
- **Analytics & reporting positioning**: Homepage cites "extensive reporting and analytics", with help-center guidance on HR analytics/automation ("Insights for a People-Centric HR: Unveiling Analytics & Automation").

### Onboarding & Recruitment (Candidate Management)
- **Structured onboarding record**: Captures Date of Joining, Recruitment Method (source tracking), offer-letter issuance, background-check information, appointment letter, and probation period per candidate.
- **Pre-joining documentation**: Handles all paperwork before day one — background-check information and mandatory documentation uploaded, stored centrally, and monitored against each candidate.
- **Offer & appointment letter generation**: Letters are generated directly on the portal from the Admin Section (a "Generate" link opens a pop-up to generate and download).
- **Onboarding checklists/tasks**: Preassigned onboarding tasks with deadlines; HR monitors completion status through checklists and receives progress notifications.
- **Roost-integrated deadline tracking**: The Onboarding module integrates with Roost so all onboarding deadlines are tracked "at your fingertips" (introduced with the Candidate Grid, Oct 2022).
- **Onboarding task setup & targeting**: Tasks are configured under Setup Policies > Onboarding and Exit > Onboarding Tasks and can target the candidate or the candidate's reporting roles/managers (direct or non-direct reporting roles).
- **Policy-driven auto-assignment**: Onboarding tasks are bundled into multi-select Onboarding policies attached to Master Policies and auto-assigned when a candidate is added to the Candidate Grid.
- **Candidate-facing Onboarding tab**: The candidate's dashboard lists assigned onboarding tasks the candidate must complete.
- **Candidate access restriction (Apr 2024)**: Candidates' access to the Roost, Profile, and Letters sections was deliberately curbed to protect sensitive data.
- **One-click candidate confirmation**: Marking a candidate "Confirmed" with a single click migrates them from Candidate Grid to Employee Grid with defined access rights and permissions.
- **Single-click candidate dossier**: Centralized view of a candidate's background verification data, offer letter, appointment letter, and onboarding tasks.
- **No standalone ATS**: The site explicitly states an applicant-tracking-system integration is not currently offered; recruitment is handled inside the Onboarding module via the Candidate Grid (SoftwareFinder's "Recruitment & Applicant Tracking" module claim is listing-only, not on the official site).

### Attendance Management
- **Capture methods**
  - **Web punch-in/punch-out**: Punch buttons in the top-right of the web portal (button turns red after punch-in, blue after punch-out), backed by "powerful rule sets" from the attendance policy.
  - **Mobile app punch**: Punch buttons at top-right of the app, with device GPS recorded if organization policy requires it.
  - **Office-location selection at punch**: At each punch the employee selects the office location from which attendance is being marked, supporting multi-site and remote/hybrid attendance (no separate WFH-request type is documented — remote work is handled via location-tagged punches).
  - **Notes/call-outs on punch**: Optional notes while punching in (e.g., client visits and locations) on both web and mobile.
  - **Biometric device integration**: Biometric devices integrate seamlessly into the portal to push logs (eSSL and Smart Office brands cited as compatible).
  - **Geofencing (Location Master)**: Geographic geofences restrict where punches are accepted (requires device location permission), marketed as preventing buddy punching.
  - **IP restriction (IP Master)**: Punch-in/out and portal login can be restricted to specific networks/IP addresses.
  - **Single vs. multiple punch mode**: A "Single Punch In" toggle allows or disables multiple punch entries; multiple-punch mode tracks entries/exits and breaks through the day.
  - **Break deduction**: A "Consider breaks in Calculation" option reduces total working hours by break duration when multiple punches are enabled.
  - **Attendance log/history**: Daily attendance log and an Attendance Calendar visible to the employee on web and mobile ("Logs" tab).
- **Policies, grace & late/early rules**
  - **Attendance cycle & minimum working hours**: The attendance policy defines the attendance cycle, shift details, minimum working hours and more.
  - **Default policies + policy builder**: Out-of-the-box basic policies covering typical rules, plus a "Powerful Policy Builder" for multiple customised rulesets applied per employee group.
  - **Round-off rules**: Round-off of employee working hours to simplify payroll calculation.
  - **Grace in Working Hours**: Minutes added to daily hours when an employee works less than required — configurable grace duration, number of grace days allowed per month, and a recurring on/off option.
  - **Flexi Punch-in Time (late-mark rules)**: Configurable flexi start time, flexi duration allowed, and number of flexi days allowed (e.g., punch between 9:15–9:30 AM up to 3 times/month).
  - **Exceed actions**: When grace or flexi limits are exceeded, an automatic action fires — Mark Half Day, Mark Absent, Mark Present, or Mark Early.
  - **Hard-limit punch-in time**: A hard cut-off (e.g., after 9:30 AM attendance is directly marked Half Day) with its own separate action.
- **Overtime**
  - **Overtime rule bases**: Overtime rules defined per Daily, Weekly, or Calculation by Pay Period.
  - **Overtime compensation options**: Overtime can be compensated as Comp Off credit or Encashment per company policy.
  - **Overtime payouts in payroll**: Overtime payouts (plus one-time payouts/deductions) feed into payroll processing before each run, with overtime components flagged in the Allowance Master.
- **Regularization**
  - **Regularization requests**: For forgotten punch-outs, out-on-duty days, or lost access cards; initiated from a "Regularize" button above the Attendance Calendar or a per-date link (web and mobile).
  - **Request fields**: Corrected In-Time and Out-Time, an organization-defined "Reason" dropdown, and free-text comments.
  - **Approval flow**: Routes to HR Head / Reporting Manager / Supervisor for approve/reject (rejection can carry a stated reason), with status tracked in a "Requests" tab.
  - **Payroll-lock window**: Once the month is closed and payroll processed, regularization requests can no longer be submitted for that period.
- **Approvals, reports & integration**
  - **Automated approval workflows**: Attendance requests run through configured hierarchies to speed up and add transparency to the process.
  - **Attendance heatmaps & metrics**: Visual attendance heatmaps of punch trends plus real-time metrics on average in/out times, deficit working hours, locations, and presence summaries on one screen.
  - **Excel + visual reporting**: Real-time data with Excel and visual reports and dashboards for HR leadership at employee/department/company level.
  - **Payroll integration**: Attendance (hours, overtime, half-days, absences) flows directly into TeamNest payroll for wage calculation.
  - **Role-based access control**: Administrative attendance functions (approvals, modifications) restricted to authorized roles.
  - **Cloud record retention**: Centralized, permanent historical attendance/leave records ("secure, transparent, permanent").

### Shift Management & Rosters
- **Shift configuration**: Create and modify multiple shifts per employee applicability; two shift types — "Start Time–End Time" (fixed window, e.g., 9:30–5:30) or "Working Hours" (fixed daily hours, e.g., 9h, with flexible start/end).
- **Pre-shift/post-shift windows**: Configurable durations before shift start and after shift end during which punches are accepted.
- **Weekly and monthly rosters**: Roster management for weekly and monthly scheduling, with bulk xlsx upload via the Setup File / Bulk Add-Update flow.
- **Master Policy assignment**: Shift, attendance, holiday-calendar, and weekly-off policies are bundled into a Master Policy and assigned to employees via the Employee Grid.

### Leave Management
- **Leave types & policy configuration**
  - **Custom leave types**: Leaves customised per company policy and employee applicability (e.g., Privilege/Earned, Sick, Casual categories chosen at application time).
  - **Leave calendar cycle**: Yearly leave cycle configurable as calendar year (Jan–Dec) or financial year (Apr–Mar).
  - **Per-employment-status quotas**: Separate leave counts for Confirmed, Trainee, and Probation employees.
  - **Accrual options (3 types)**: "In advance for the current period" (full-year credit up front), "Earned as you go along" (cumulative, typically monthly), or "Earned for the past period" (credited at period end), with monthly/quarterly/yearly frequencies varied per leave type.
  - **Carry-forward**: Balance carry-forward with a maximum threshold, supporting intra-cycle or yearly carry-forward of unutilized leaves.
  - **Encashment**: Balance encashment to monetize unused leave per policy, tying into payroll.
  - **Negative balance**: An "Allow Negative Balance" option permits applying beyond current balance.
  - **Half-day leave**: A per-policy toggle allowing half-days on leave applications.
  - **Request caps**: "Maximum days per leave request" and "Maximum requests per month" limits.
  - **Prefix/Postfix/Sandwich rules**: Define whether week-offs/holidays adjacent to (before, after, or between) leave days are deducted from the balance.
  - **Proxy leave applications**: Supervisors/HR/admins can raise leave requests on behalf of employees.
  - **Leave page visibility control**: Configure whether employees see leave request/balance status.
  - **Mandatory rejection reason**: Policy can require reporting managers to give a reason when rejecting leave.
- **Application & approval workflow**
  - **Self-service leave application**: Employee selects leave type, from/to dates, adds comments, uploads supporting documents, and can enter an alternate contact number, alternate address, and an alternate employee to contact during absence.
  - **Parallel or sequential approval hierarchies**: Custom multi-level workflows for leave approval or rejection.
  - **Notifications + email**: Reporting Manager is notified on submission; the employee gets in-app notification and email on approval/rejection with reasons.
  - **Status tracking & balances**: A "Leave Application Status" tab shows Pending/Approved/Rejected, with balances per leave type shown on web and mobile.
  - **Mobile leave approvals**: Managers approve/reject leave and other requests from the mobile app.
- **Leave Planner & Team Calendar**
  - **Leave Planner (tentative leaves)**: Employees log tentative vacation dates to inform the Reporting Manager in advance; a planned leave is explicitly not an actual leave request.
  - **Convert plan to request**: Finalized plans convert to an actual leave request with the application form pre-filled.
  - **Team Calendar**: HR managers/supervisors view all team members' planned leave in a single screen to prevent bunching/overlapping leaves and arrange shift coverage.
- **Reports & integrations**
  - **Leave reports & analytics**: Comprehensive reports and visual data on leave types taken, available balances, and approved requests; real-time access to accruals and balances with alerts for exceptional cases (e.g., employees nearing leave exhaustion).
  - **Leave–payroll integration**: The system automatically calculates leaves per month per employee and applies the paid-leave/LOP impact to total compensation and pay.
  - **Slack / Microsoft Teams integration**: Leave data/requests can be integrated with Slack or Microsoft Teams for notifications.
  - **Noted gap (vendor-documented)**: TeamNest documentation states the system currently lacks automatic absence-to-leave deduction functionality.

### Holidays, Weekly-Offs, Optional Holidays & Comp-Off
- **Holiday Master**: Add holidays singly (Name, Date, Type) or via bulk upload; each holiday is typed "Fixed" (compulsory) or "Optional".
- **Multiple holiday calendars**: Multiple calendars (with a pre-populated standard-holiday list to assign from) applied to different employee groups/locations/entities via Master Policy.
- **Optional holidays with quota**: An "Allow Optional Holidays" toggle plus a maximum-per-calendar-year quota; employees apply with leave type "Optional Holiday", the system blocks non-designated dates and over-quota requests with errors, the balance shows in the leave counter, and manager approval fires notification + email.
- **Weekly-off policies**: Choose applicable days from all seven weekdays (e.g., all Saturdays and Sundays), with multiple weekly-off settings for different employee groups assigned via Master Policy.
- **Comp-off credit requests**: An "Add New (Credit)" request type where the employee submits a comp-off credit claim for approval.
- **Comp-off debit requests**: A "Subtract From Existing (Debit)" request type where the employee picks a previously credited comp-off date to consume it.
- **Comp-off from overtime**: Overtime rules can pay out as comp-off credit as an alternative to encashment.

### Payroll Processing Engine
- **Payroll Wizard (guided run)**: A step-by-step wizard ("Run payroll anytime by yourself") walking admins through period/employee selection, attendance confirmation, one-time payouts/deductions, and the final run, via Admin Section > Payroll > View/Run Payroll.
- **Selective payroll runs**: Run payroll for the entire organization, individual departments/OUs, or specific employee sets; a "Run Again" option re-runs payroll for one or more employees after corrections.
- **Batch payroll processing**: Payroll processed in smaller batches by Organizational Unit (Department, Sub-company, Division) with separate attendance cycles; employee lists can be downloaded, filtered, and re-uploaded to create custom batches (July–Aug 2025 update).
- **Custom payable-days basis**: Payroll computed on a custom number of days rather than the calendar month, aligned to attendance-cycle cut-off dates, automatically picking up employees whose Date of Joining/Termination falls inside the cycle.
- **Pending-request gate**: The wizard surfaces all pending Leave and Attendance Regularisation requests for the pay period and forces approval/rejection before payroll proceeds.
- **Attendance confirmation & override**: Daily attendance for all employees is shown for confirmation; individual day records can be edited, and external attendance/leave data can be uploaded to override TeamNest's own records as the payroll basis.
- **Adjustment days for prior months**: Employee records edited during the run can add prior-period day arrears; adjustment days also factor into HRA-exemption and statutory-bonus computations.
- **One-Time Payouts (Excel upload)**: Ad-hoc payments (e.g., quarterly/yearly components falling due) injected via an Excel upload step; one-off items can also be added directly onto an individual payslip.
- **One-Time Deductions incl. statutory arrears**: Ad-hoc deductions via Excel; the arrears upload supports all statutory deductions (PF, PT, ESIC, LWF, not just TDS).
- **Increments upload**: The wizard's Increments tab accepts an Excel to assign/change increments, with a Designation column so promotions and appraised CTC update together.
- **Payroll lock**: Prevents running the current month's payroll unless previous months are locked, protecting past records from unintended alteration.
- **Payroll audit trail**: Audit Trails automatically log changes to Allowances/Deductions and every step of a payroll re-run.
- **Overtime payments**: Overtime payment file processing is a supported payroll input.
- **Performance-optimized processing**: Payroll processing sped up ~5x; large registers (e.g., a 5,000-employee Salary Register) generate in under a minute, with background "Downloaded Reports" processing so admins can keep working.

### Salary Structures & CTC Components
- **CTC Masters (Define Masters)**: Master lists of everything composing CTC — CTC Items (default buckets: Gross, Retirals, Reimbursements, Other Benefits), Allowances, Deductions, Loans, and Statutory Deductions.
- **Allowance master with frequency & payout type**: Each allowance has a Frequency (Monthly, Quarterly, Yearly, or Informational — only Monthly items pay in the regular cycle; others sit in CTC and are paid via One-Time Payout), a Payout Type (Pro-Rata by days worked vs Fixed), a CTC-item grouping, and per-allowance ESIC/PF/PT/TDS applicability flags.
- **Deduction master**: Custom deductions (defaults include VPF and Full and Final Settlement) with the same Frequency/Payout-Type/CTC-grouping model.
- **CTC Builder / salary structures**: Structures combine allowances, deductions, and statutory deductions; each component is a fixed value or a percentage of another field (e.g., HRA as % of Basic), and multiple CTC rule structures can be auto-applied to new joiners for automatic CTC break-up.
- **Structure assignment (individual & bulk)**: Assign CTC to an individual (structure + actual amount), Bulk Upload CTC Using Salary Structure (Excel), or Bulk Upload CTC Using Breakup (per-employee break-up without a structure).
- **Per-employee overrides**: "Assign Allowance" and "Assign Deduction" attach individual components to one employee, overriding their assigned structure.
- **Statutory deduction settings per structure**: Structures declare which statutory deductions apply; PF supports a designated percentage deduction, and system-defined allowances (Basic, DA for PF; Education Allowance, HRA etc. for TDS) are selected by default and cannot be removed (statutory guardrail, Jan–Mar 2026 update).
- **Pension (EPS) exemption flag**: Employees exempt from the Employer's Pension Scheme can be flagged individually in the profile or via Bulk Upload Employee Master.
- **Allowance Master Report**: Downloadable report of all active allowances with frequency, payout type, CTC grouping, overtime flag, and ESIC/PF/PT/TDS applicability.

### Payslips & Employee Payroll Access
- **Online payslips**: Payslips publish to the portal after each run with instant self-service access (payslips, tax computations, Form 16) from ESS "My Folder" and the mobile app.
- **Payslip upload email notifications**: Employees receive an automatic email when payslips are uploaded.
- **Password-protected payslips & FFS slips**: Document security settings (Admin > Payroll Utilities > Document Security) add password protection to payslips and Full & Final Settlement slips.
- **Bulk/combined payslip downloads**: Payslips downloadable combined for all OUs or per-OU ("Combined All OUs Beta" / "Individual All OUs Beta"); payslip + tax computation download as a single combined file for selected OUs/employees, and single-employee downloads come as PDF instead of ZIP.
- **Year-to-Date (YTD) payslips**: For a mid-year leaver with processed FFS, all payslips for the year plus the FFS slip are retrievable in one click from Registers and Checklists.

### Expense Management, Travel & Reimbursements
- **Four out-of-the-box expense categories**: Business Expense, Local Travel Expense, Trip Expense (outstation), and Flexi Expense, plus custom category creation.
- **Trip-based travel expenses**: Create a "Trip" and add sub-claims incrementally on the go under subcategories (Travel, Accommodation, Others), saving as incurred and submitting as one unit.
- **Flexi reimbursements in CTC**: Computation of flexi reimbursement amounts under heads like Mobile, Fuel, Driver's Salary as tax-saving CTC components, with a Flexi Yearly Report cross-tabbing entitlement usage per employee per month.
- **Reimbursement Slip**: Downloadable/publishable slip showing reimbursement components with applicable rates, opening balance, entitlement amount, passing amount, current bills, closing balance, and bills carried forward from the previous cycle.
- **Mobile receipt capture & location**: Photograph and upload bills/receipts from the app, with Google-location pickup for local travel claims.
- **Approval workflow**: Requests route to the Reporting Manager through single or multi-level, parallel or sequential hierarchies, with rejection reasons recorded and bulk approve/reject/modify of multiple requests at once.
- **Status tracking**: A "My Expenses" tab shows Approved, Rejected, and Pending Reimbursement requests.
- **Payment mode & currency capture**: Employees record the mode of payment and currency type used.
- **Mileage policy**: Default compensation per kilometre for self-owned vehicle fuel, set in Expense Configuration.
- **GST/input-tax-credit tracking**: Taxes (e.g., multiple GST rates) defined and mapped per category so the organization can view tax set-off/input-credit amounts embedded in reimbursements, with automatic tax adjustments over categorized expense heads.
- **Expense restriction rules**: Policy limits on back-dated expense applications and cancellations.
- **Expense analytics**: Month-to-month expense comparison, pending/approved summaries, and visual and tabular reports (including a "Bucket" column in Expense Request & Summary reports).
- **Payroll linkage & paid status**: Expense records flow seamlessly into the Payroll module for reimbursement; employees see paid-expense details and an Unpaid Expense Report supports reconciliation.
- **No travel booking**: No dedicated corporate travel-booking (flights/hotels) capability is mentioned anywhere.

### Loans & Advances
- **Loan Master**: Named loans defined as EMI-based or non-EMI-based in the payroll masters.
- **Automatic EMI recovery**: Automatic monthly deduction of loan installments and applicable interest during payroll.
- **Future-dated installment start**: Loans added with installment deductions commencing on a future date.
- **Loan perquisite valuation**: Perquisite tax on subsidized/interest-free loans computed from the prevalent interest rate keyed to the sanction date, handling future sanction dates within the FY.
- **Loan Register**: Payroll register detailing loans, including Pre-Payment / Top-Up amounts and EMI details.

### Arrears, Bonus, Increments & Vouchers
- **Arrears handling**: Prior-month days added as adjustment days in the run; arrears of statutory deductions (PF, PT, ESIC, LWF, TDS) uploaded through the one-time deductions file.
- **Statutory Bonus Report**: Bonus computation report under Statutory Reports (Payment of Bonus Act 1965 coverage), including adjustment days along with current-month days.
- **Increment Report / Reconciliation Register**: Reconciliation Registers show increment details and month-on-month CTC comparison (current vs previous month for Gross, Net, PF, TDS) highlighting CTC changes for validation; the increment report includes statutory components (PF Capped, PF Uncapped, ESIC) when part of CTC.
- **Voucher payments**: Miscellaneous voucher payments (Add Single Voucher) with duplicate-entry validation on Employee + Payment Date + Payment Head, plus a financial-year-to-date Voucher Payment Register.
- **Journal Voucher Register**: Generates salary-related journal entries in Excel for import into accounting software.

### Full & Final Settlement (F&F)
- **Process FFS inside payroll**: A "Process FFS" checkbox in the payroll wizard runs full & final settlement for exiting employees; FFS also exists as a default deduction head in the masters.
- **Exit-module linkage**: The Exit Grid tracks Relieving Date, Exit Reason, asset recovery, and an FFS Status field HR marks Completed — deliberately decoupled from the actual payroll-side FFS processing.
- **FNF Register**: The month's FNF register can be generated in Salary Register format via an "All FNF Employees" dropdown.
- **FFS slip delivery to personal email**: Because official email is deactivated at exit, admins can download and email the FFS slip plus tax computation to the leaver's personal/alternate email ID.
- **FFS slip password protection**: The same document-security password scheme as payslips.
- **Leave encashment report**: Report of accumulated leave-encashment amounts per employee, feeding settlement/benefits computations.

### Statutory Compliance — PF, ESI, PT, LWF
- **In-built statutory engine**: Default provisions for ESIC, Profession Tax, Provident Fund, and TDS, with per-allowance applicability mapping deciding what each statutory head is computed on; PF percentage is configurable and PF validation logic is rate-based rather than pro-rata-amount-based.
- **PF forms & challans**: Preparation of PF Forms 2, 9, 10C, 11, and 19; system-generated PF Form 5 and Form 10 data (new joinees, nominees, withdrawal requests of exited employees) in the Challans section; EDLI and administrative fee management.
- **ESIC compliance**: ESIC compilation, ECR generation, and medical-records preparation.
- **Professional Tax (state-wise)**: PT setup per applicable state with Monthly/Half-Yearly/Annual deposition tracking; PT slabs updated per state notifications (e.g., Maharashtra, Karnataka), a "State of PT" field in bulk employee upload, and a PT Report among payroll outputs.
- **Labour Welfare Fund**: Automated LWF deduction processing, state-wise applicability setup, return-filing guidance, and a Monthly LWF Report showing employee deductions plus company-side contributions.
- **Employer pension-fund perquisite (₹7.5 lakh rule)**: Employer PF + NPS + Superannuation contributions above ₹7,50,000/year are automatically added to income as a taxable perquisite.
- **Compliance Calendar**: Calendar interface of due dates for each enabled compliance (PF, ESIC, PT, TDS, LWF) with challan/acknowledgement upload (filing date and comments), colour coding (orange due, green on-time, yellow late), multi-compliance day drill-down, and later download of uploaded documents.
- **Extended compliance coverage**: Custom Compliance Masters (per sub-company), Compliance Events with Monthly/Quarterly/Half-Yearly/Yearly frequencies and reminders, Maker–Checker workflows (employee maker workspace, supervisor checker approval of challans), an employee-facing Compliance Dashboard, coverage widened beyond payroll acts to ROC, MGT, CSR, SBO, POSH, Payment of Bonus Act 1965, and Minimum Wage Act 1948, and unclaimed-wages register maintenance.

### TDS, Income Tax & Form 16
- **TDS allowance mapping**: Admins move allowance heads between "Remaining Allowances" and "Allowance Under TDS" lists so payroll computes tax only on mapped taxable allowances.
- **Old vs New Tax Regime handling**: New Regime is the default per FY; employees switch to Old Regime via a dropdown with on-screen acknowledgement or a signed Section 115BAC declaration upload (available to new joiners for 30 days from DOJ); once switched to Old Regime the employee cannot revert within the FY, and 80G is disabled under the New Regime.
- **Tax Calculator (ESS)**: Employee-side what-if calculator comparing Old vs New Regime side-by-side, pre-filled from provisional (or, in Q4, approved actual) declarations, updated for each Union Budget.
- **Section 87A rebate visibility**: Rebate u/s 87A shown explicitly in Tax Computations, the Income Tax Details Report, and the e-TDS salary sheet.
- **HRA exemption workings**: Tax computation includes an HRA-exemption footnote and an annual rent break-up table in the Tax Projection report; HRA exemption considers prior-month adjustment/repayment days.
- **Tax Computation sheets**: Per-employee tax computation uploads/downloads with batch download for all employees in one background job (no 100-employee batch limit), resilient to page closure.
- **Tax Projection & Income Tax Detail reports**: Include employee Work Type (Full-time, Contract, Professional, etc.) since it affects tax rules, with a dedicated Income Tax Detail Report for resigned employees.
- **e-TDS / quarterly returns**: e-TDS report output including an optimized Q4 eTDS report with yearly earnings and tax computations; eTDS Yearly Report validations accommodate resigned employees, and Form 24 preparation is listed under the compliance offering.
- **Form 16 & annexures**: Form 16 with annexures generated/distributed via the portal — bulk digitally-signed Form 16 generation "with a single click" via the myitreturn partnership (offered as a free tool) — plus an admin-generated "Annexure to Form 16" giving allowance-wise gross break-up and Section 10 exemption details.
- **Form 12BA**: Statement of perquisites/fringe benefits (fuel, driver's salary, accommodation, etc.) generated for all applicable employees alongside Form 16.
- **Previous-employer income**: Declaration sections capture Previous Employer Income Details and Income from Other Sources — the only sections permitted negative amounts.

### Investment Declarations & Proof Submission
- **Provisional declaration (ESS)**: Employees enter declared amounts against a list of investment items from My Folder > Investment Declaration, with Save as Draft (multi-session, no HR notification) vs Save & Submit (notifies HR/Admin), editable until the admin locks submission.
- **Actual proofs with attachments**: From January onward employees upload PDF/image proofs; sections include 80C-type items, 80D, HRA rent, housing-loan details (Self-Occupied vs Let-Out property, Joint House Ownership document, Loss from House Property document, Lender PAN and Name), and perquisites.
- **Audit/approve workflow**: Admin/auditor console (Payroll Utilities > Investments) with Pending/Draft filters, per-item approved amounts, notes to employees on partial approval/rejection, "Mark as Audited", notifications both ways, and automatic case re-open if the employee edits after audit.
- **Four control toggles**: "Disable Provisional Investments", "Enable Actual Investments (With Proof)", "Consider Actual Investments" (TDS on approved amounts), and "Always Consider Actual Investments for March Payroll" (now locked ON by default so March payroll always uses audited proofs).
- **Copy-over provisional→approved**: One-click auto-copy of declared amounts to approved amounts (e.g., House Rent paid to Landlord) to skip manual re-entry when proofs are enabled.
- **Bulk investment uploads & reports**: Bulk Upload Investment Declaration and Bulk Upload Investment with Proof files (including a 'Perquisites on Others' column), a crosstab Excel of declared vs approved amounts, an "Employees with No Investment" report, a proof-submission status flag in Investment Reports, and negative-amount validations.

### Bank Transfer & Payment Outputs
- **Bank Transfer File**: After the run is reviewed and closed, a Bank Transfer File is generated from View/Run Payroll to effect salary transfers (no named bank APIs — file-based).
- **Bank data capture**: Employee bank details in ESS with supporting-document upload (passbook/cancelled cheque), a dynamically generated Bank Master list of bank names, an IFSC Code head in CTC & Salary Registers, and Bank Branch Name in Salary and Salary+CTC registers.
- **Payment Register**: Listed among standard post-payroll outputs alongside the Salary Register.

### Gratuity

### Payroll Reports & Registers
- **Standard register set**: CTC Register, Salary Register, Salary plus CTC Register, Payment Register, Form II (Rule 27(1)), Loan Register, PT Report, PF Report, ESIC Report, Income Tax Report, e-TDS Report, and Statutory Reports, generated under Registers/Checklists after each run.
- **CTC Register – Annual sheet**: A separate sheet with annual values of every allowance and statutory deduction (PF, ESIC) for a total-compensation view; CTC Master and CTC Register include deduction heads (LIC premium, Health Insurance), and the CTC Master export is filterable by employee status (Active, On Hold, Resigned…).
- **Consolidated Salary Register**: Combines data from the start of the financial year to date (auditor-oriented), with historical/backdated CTC Registers and Tax Computations also accessible.
- **Payment Details – Employee Wise Report**: Per-employee view of specific allowances/deductions paid in the current financial year.
- **Reconciliation Register**: Multi-sheet month-on-month comparison (Gross, Net, PF, TDS) plus increment details and CTC-change highlighting.
- **Register enrichments**: UAN Number column in the Salary Register, a Total Deductions (statutory + non-statutory) column, sub-company names in all payroll registers/statutory reports with standard file naming, resignation dates in the CTC Report, and Middle Name/Relation fields as required in PF reports.
- **Background report generation ("Downloaded Reports")**: Long-running reports (Salary/CTC/Salary+CTC registers, tax computations) generate in the background with status tracking — a 1,000-employee Salary+CTC register completes in under 2 minutes.
- **Group/multi-domain reporting**: Combined CTC Master report across group domains from the Master Admin login, with a "Select All domains" option for payroll reports in the Master Dashboard.
- **Report access control**: Restriction Access Control limits which payroll/attendance reports each Admin/HR can see (need-to-know, including per-OU read/write vs read-only settings); Downloaded Reports access is separately grantable, and report output columns are customizable to restrict shared data.

### Performance Management (PMS)

- **Configurable review cycles**: Monthly, Quarterly, Half-Yearly, and Annual review cycles with administrator-set start/end dates and the ability to extend the review period.

- **Employee self-assessment**: Employees rate themselves on assigned KRAs/competencies in the "KRAs & Competencies" section, add comments, and upload supporting documents.

- **Custom approval workflows for reviews**: Complex, org-specific performance-review approval workflows (multi-step approval of ratings) can be configured.
- **PMS analytics**: KRA and Competency Summary Reports, Balance Check reports, and Cycle Review Reports to track performance trends.

### Helpdesk (Inter-Department HR Ticketing)
- **Purpose & positioning**
  - **Inter-department ticketing platform**: Officially defined as "an inter-department ticketing platform in Teamnest for resolving work-related issues raised by employees" — not HR-only, covering Operations, HR, Marketing, IT, and Finance/Accounts.
  - **Single window for employee concerns**: Marketed as a centralised solution for all employee and company interactions, explicitly positioned to eliminate email-based query/response threads and to track open issues to reduce response times.
- **Employee flow**
  - **"Raise New Ticket" form**: Opened from the left navigation (web) or the Helpdesk dashboard icon (mobile), with a required Category/Department selection driving routing plus a short free-text description.
  - **Document attachment**: Employees can optionally attach a document to clarify the issue, on both web and mobile.
  - **Ticket List status tracking**: Employees monitor ticket status from a "Ticket List" section on web and in the app.
  - **Threaded conversation**: When a ticket is responded to, the employee is notified, reads the response, and can write back — the conversation continues until the issue is resolved.
- **Admin configuration & routing**
  - **Fully customizable categories**: Issue categories are entirely customizable (examples: IT, Accounts, HR), added via Admin Section > Setup Policies > Helpdesk > Add Category.
  - **Category Owner assignment**: One person per category is selected as responsible for all its tickets ("Assign Owners"), and owners must be assigned to all categories before the module is ready.
  - **Automatic category-based routing**: Based on the selected category, the request auto-routes to the designated owner (the relevant Functional Head).
  - **Helpdesk rule inside Master Policy**: Helpdesk is one of the seven mandatory Master Policy rule sections, applied to employees, an Org Unit, or an Org Group.
- **Ownership, reassignment & resolution**
  - **Owner notification on every event**: Owners are notified each time a request is raised and when the employee messages on the ticket.
  - **Priority-based working**: Owners work toward resolution "as per the priority" (priority as a working order; no configurable priority-level scheme is documented).
  - **Supervisor/Manager reassignment**: The supervisor/manager can reassign or redirect a ticket to a different owner as needed; the reassignment picker currently lists all employees with no department-specific filtering (documented limitation).
  - **Closure by Category Owner**: The Category Owner marks the ticket Closed — the documented terminal status — triggering a notification to the employee.
- **Notifications & mobile**
  - **Three documented notification triggers**: Ticket creation (to owner), responses in both directions, and closure.
  - **Full module in the mobile app**: The entire Helpdesk module is accessible in the app, whose App Store description lists "Ticket-based support" as a core capability, with a mobile flow mirroring web.
- **Verified absences (not documented anywhere official or third-party)**
  - **No SLA/TAT engine**: No SLA timers, turnaround-time targets, or response/resolution deadlines documented.
  - **No escalation matrix**: No auto-escalation levels or multi-tier routing; the only reassignment path is manual redirect.
  - **No helpdesk analytics/reports**: No resolution-time reporting, ticket-volume dashboards, CSAT surveys, or Helpdesk-specific MIS exports documented.
  - **No configurable priorities or intermediate statuses**: Only "Closed" is named as a status; no Open/In-Progress/On-Hold taxonomy or selectable priority field.
  - **Single owner per category**: Exactly one responsible person per category; no team queues, round-robin, or multi-agent assignment documented.
